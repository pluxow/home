Last updated: April 2026

EPM APP ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

WhatsApp Business Data (V4 API EPM)

We collect WhatsApp Business Account data including phone number, profile name, and message content/metadata for the purpose of providing customer service messaging through V4 API EPM. Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and is not sold to third parties.

  • Phone number: The WhatsApp Business phone number connected via Embedded Signup.
  • Profile name: The display name of your WhatsApp Business account.
  • Message metadata: Timestamps, delivery status, and message content processed to operate the service.
  • WABA ID and Phone Number ID: Stored to maintain your API connection.

To request deletion of your WhatsApp data, email leandro.couretot@pluxow.com with subject "Eliminar datos WhatsApp". Requests are processed within 72 hours.
